About Vancouver

Where to stay

Vancouver is busy and expensive, especially in the summer. Make sure you give yourself plenty of time to find a place to stay.

If you're travelling with your family or are comfortable splitting a home with a group of friends, Airbnb may be a good option for you.

Looking for less hassle and full service? The wedding is downtown so there are plenty of hotels to choose from. On a budget? Save some money and stay at the Jericho Beach Hostel. There is also nearby camping at Capilano River or at Burnaby Moutain.

Getting around

There's no Uber here! That shouldn't be a problem though, plenty of Vancouver is very accessible by foot, bike or public transit. If you plan on using public transit more than once, it's worth picking up a Compass Card. Otherwise, booking a cab is usually fairly reliable using the Yellow Cab App on Android or iOS. There's also Mobi Bike Sharing.

What's happening

There will be a number of events happening in the city. On Friday night Davie Street will be shut down for Vancouver Pride events. On Saturday night Pacific Blvd will be closed for the Honda Celebration of Light. Over 400,000 people come to English Bay to watch the show. Finally, on Sunday (the day of the wedding) the Vancouver Pride Parade will take place on Robson, Denman and Davie Street from 11-2pm. The party continues on to Sunset beach! All of these events are a ton of fun but please keep this in mind while getting around!

  • Rent a bike and tour the seawall
  • Take a walk through Stanley Park
